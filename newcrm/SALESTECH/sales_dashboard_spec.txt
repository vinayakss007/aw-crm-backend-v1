SALES DASHBOARD DATA & FEATURE SPECIFICATION

FILE NAME sales_dashboard_spec.txt

PURPOSE This document defines: - What data comes IN - What features are
shown - What data goes OUT - How raw sales data turns into business
decisions

  ------------------------------
  1. DATA IN (SOURCE OF TRUTH)
  ------------------------------

USERS (Sales Team) - user_id - name - role (rep / manager / admin) -
team_id - region - joining_date - status

LEADS - lead_id - created_at - source - industry - company_size -
owner_user_id - status

OPPORTUNITIES (Deals) - deal_id - lead_id - owner_user_id - stage -
deal_value - probability - expected_close_date - actual_close_date -
created_at - updated_at - loss_reason - product - region

ACTIVITIES - activity_id - deal_id - user_id - activity_type -
activity_date - duration_minutes - outcome

TARGETS - target_id - user_id / team_id - time_period - target_value

  -------------------------
  2. DATA TRANSFORMATIONS
  -------------------------

Closed Revenue Sum of deal_value where stage = closed_won

Pipeline Value Sum of deal_value where deal not closed

Weighted Pipeline deal_value Ã— probability

Win Rate closed_won / (closed_won + closed_lost)

Sales Velocity Average days from deal creation to close

  -------------------
  3. FEATURES SHOWN
  -------------------

EXECUTIVE VIEW - Total revenue (MTD/QTD/YTD) - Revenue vs target -
Forecast confidence - Pipeline coverage - High-risk deals

MANAGER VIEW - Funnel conversion - Rep leaderboard - Deal slippage -
Activity vs revenue

SALES REP VIEW - My open deals - Next actions - Deal risk alerts -
Activity score

  --------------------------------
  4. DATA OUT (DASHBOARD OUTPUT)
  --------------------------------

KPI Metrics - Revenue - Target - Growth rate - Win rate

Charts - Funnel chart - Revenue trend - Pipeline by stage - Rep
performance table

Alerts - Stuck deals - High-risk deals - Target gap warnings

  ------------
  5. FILTERS
  ------------

-   Date range
-   Sales rep
-   Team
-   Region
-   Product
-   Deal size
